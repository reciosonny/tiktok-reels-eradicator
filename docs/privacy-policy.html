Privacy Policy

Feed Breaker — Chrome Extension

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Overview

Feed Breaker is a Chrome extension that helps you reclaim your time by blocking addictive feed and short-form content pages. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the extension stores and how it is used.

Data We Store

Feed Breaker stores the following data locally on your device using the Chrome chrome.storage.local API:

  • Time counter — cumulative seconds saved by not scrolling through feeds.
  • Disable preferences — whether blocking is temporarily paused and for how long.
  • Custom quotes — any motivational quotes you add in the options page.

How Your Data Is Used

All stored data is used solely to power the extension's features (displaying time saved, respecting your disable settings, and showing your custom quotes). No data is processed for any other purpose.

Data Sharing

We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your data with third parties. All data remains on your device and is never sent to any external server.

Data Removal

You can clear all stored data at any time by removing the extension from Chrome or by clearing extension storage via Chrome's developer tools.
